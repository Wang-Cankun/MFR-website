Recourse

We have used MFR model to measure relatedness of the gene pairs in the DIP, geneFriends, cancer and KEGG-metabolism datasets, in which the positive samples in the DIP dataset are gene pairs with PPIs collected from the DIP database and the negative samples are gene pairs composited randomly by the genes in the positive samples, the positive samples in the geneFriends dataset are the gene pairs with co-expressions collected from the geneFriends database and the negative samples are gene pairs randomly by the genes in the positive samples, the samples in the TCGA cancer datasets are gene pairs composited by the genes up-regulated in four cancer types including BLCA, BRCA, COAD and LUAD, and the samples in KEGG-metabolism dataset are the gene pairs composited by the genes involved in metabolism pathways collected from the KEGG database. All the results mentioned above are downloaded freely for further verification and biological analysis.
